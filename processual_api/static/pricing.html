Refund terms

Refund requests are reviewed when the core operational outcome of the paid trial is not achieved. This means the program runs, the agreed in-scope tasks execute, and verifiable results are obtained inside the program.

  • Refunds do not cover the customer’s inability to connect external agents, providers, or accounts.
  • Refunds do not cover customer-side credential or configuration issues.
  • Trial expiration before the proposed usage quantity is consumed is not by itself a refund reason.
